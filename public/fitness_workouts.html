Beginner Full Body Workout

  1. Warm-up Phase
    1. 5-minute light cardio
    2. Dynamic stretching
      1. Arm circles: 30 seconds each
      2. Leg swings: 30 seconds each
      3. Torso twists: 30 seconds
    3. Joint mobilization: 2 minutes
  2. Main Workout
    1. Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
    2. Push-ups: 3 sets to failure
    3. Bent-over Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
    4. Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  3. Cool-down
    1. Static stretching: 5 minutes
    2. Deep breathing: 2 minutes

Weekly Training Schedule Structure

Day 1: Upper Body Strength
  • Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets x 10-15 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets to failure
Day 2: Lower Body Power
  • Barbell Squats: 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets x 5 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets x 12 reps each leg
Day 3: Active Recovery
  • Light cardio: 20-30 minutes
  • Mobility work
  • Foam rolling
Day 4: Full Body Circuit
  1. Kettlebell Swings: 45 seconds
  2. Push-ups: 45 seconds
  3. Bodyweight Squats: 45 seconds
  4. Rest: 60 seconds
  5. Repeat 4 rounds
